## Detailed Description

AI Content Assistant brings the power of large language models to every webpage you visit.

**How it works:**
1. Select any text on any webpage
2. Click the floating ✨ button
3. AI instantly summarizes, translates, explains, or rewrites the text

**Features:**
- 📝 **Summarize** — Condense long articles into key points in seconds
- 🌐 **Translate** — Auto-detect language, translate between Chinese and English
- 💡 **Explain** — Understand complex concepts in simple terms
- ✍️ **Rewrite** — Polish your writing with better clarity and flow

**Why this extension:**
- 🚀 Lightning fast — DeepSeek API responses in under 2 seconds
- 💰 Affordable — ~¥1 per 1M tokens, far cheaper than alternatives
- 🔒 Private — Your API key stays on your machine
- 🎯 Zero setup — Works on every website instantly

**Free Tier:** 5 AI actions per day
